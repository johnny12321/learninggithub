Welcome to my first MDX page! This document demonstrates the combination of standard Markdown with the power of JSX.

You can write regular Markdown content just like this paragraph.

Here is a simple [hyperlink to the MDX documentation](https://mdxjs.com/).

You can also use a link with an optional title that appears as a tooltip on hover, as described in the [Codecademy guide on Markdown links](https://www.codecademy.com/resources/docs/markdown/links "Learn more about MDX").
